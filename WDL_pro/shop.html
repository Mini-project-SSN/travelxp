Must Visit Places for Any Shop-a-holic

1. New Market, Kolkata

Book your tickets here!

This market in Kolkata was earlier known as Hogg Saheber Bazaar. It offers a wide range of clothes, jewellery, make-up, meat and poultry making it a one stop shop for Pujo, wedding shopping and shopping in general.

Already luring to fill your shopping bags with the best and the cheapest material? The next time when you are traveling, just keep in mind that beautiful sceneries aren’t enough to suffice your trip. Unless you bring back home various souvenirs and cultural artefacts from the various cities, your trip lies incomplete!

Other places to shop are Dakshinapan Shopping Center and Chowringh Road. Other than the iconic lal paar saris with red borders , Kolkata can surprise with immense variety in silk, Tussar etc as well. Don't leave without Terracotta handicrafts, sweets like Sandesh and Chumchum and a stop at the College street for chancing upon some rare books.

2. Sarojini Market, Delhi

Book your tickets here!

A number of Bollywood songs dedicated to it and a number of videos on the social media have provided Sarojini Market with all the hype that this street market deserves. Ask any Delhiite and they will tell you what Sarojini market means to them. Sarojini market sells the export surplus material at a very cheap price.

This flea market is probably the best street market that you will find in the capital city. The market is always filled with the recent fashion clothes, bags, and shoes. Visit here after improvising your bargaining skills, because it can land you with the most awesome articles at the dirt cheap rate.

3. Colaba Causeway, Mumbai

Book your tickets here!

Colaba Causeway is the most sought after street market in Mumbai which is jam packed with not just the local people but with the fashionistas of the town too. You can totally trust this high-fashion market of Mumbai to keep your wardrobe up to date without really hurting your wallet.

Colaba Causeway offers everything from the garment store to the stylish handbags and shoes, the local food and the roadside jewelry stalls. Fill your shopping bag with best of material at the totally affordable price.

4. Hyderabad - The City of Pearls

Book your tickets here!

The Charminar at Hyderabad stands in a labyrinth of lanes crammed with shops, markets, stalls and shoppers - one of the best places to shop for the authentic pearls and relish the tasty biryanis, skewer kebabs and haleem. You'll love Hyderabad if you're a shopaholic.

From Pearls, Ittar, Pochampally Ikkat Sarees, Mojaris to Kalamkari fabrics, Bidri work, Lac Bangles, you'll find it all. The top markets here are Char Minar Bazaar, Laad Bazaar, Koti Market
